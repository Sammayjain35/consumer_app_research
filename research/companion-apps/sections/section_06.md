### Search Trends (Google Trends, India)

Keywords tracked: **AI girlfriend, AI companion, Replika, Wysa, Ira** — geo: India

**"AI girlfriend" dominates — and reveals real demand framing.** Users in India are searching for relationship-style AI, not wellness tools. The word "companion" is clinical; "girlfriend" is what they actually type. Top related queries: "ai chat," "best ai girlfriend app," "ai girlfriend video."

**Grok is the biggest rising signal in this entire category.** Rising queries for "AI girlfriend": *grok ai* (+126,950%), *grok ai girlfriend* (+400%). For "AI companion": *grok ai companion* (+392,850%), *grok companion* (+390,600%). Grok is entering this space with massive tailwind — none of the incumbent apps (Replika, Chai, Rumik) have a comparable search surge.

**"AI companion" searches don't mean what you'd expect.** Top queries: "Zoom AI companion," "Microsoft your AI companion," "AI companion in Zoom." In India, searching "AI companion" mostly captures enterprise productivity intent, not emotional app intent. The emotional demand lives under "AI girlfriend," not "AI companion."

**Regional breakdown — two distinct geographies:**

| Wysa | Karnataka (2), Haryana (2), Delhi (2) | Minimal — despite being India-origin |
| Ira (Rumik) | Dominated by "Roth IRA" and "Ira Khan" — near-zero brand signal | Rumik's brand name has no search identity in India |

**Three implications:**
1. Rumik's WhatsApp-native distribution works *because* it bypasses search — but it also means zero organic discovery outside their existing network.
2. Wysa's low search presence in India despite being India-built suggests their marketing is B2B/enterprise focused, not consumer.
3. The real battlefield for consumer AI companions in India is the "AI girlfriend" keyword cluster — which no Indian startup currently owns in paid or organic search.
